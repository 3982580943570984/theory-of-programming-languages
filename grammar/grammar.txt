<Program>                     ::= <Variable Declaration> <Description of Computations>

<Variable Declaration>        ::= VAR <List of Variables> : INTEGER ;
<List of Variables>           ::= <Identifier> | <Identifier>,<List of Variables>
<Identifier>                  ::= <Letter> <Identifier> | <Letter>
<Letter>                      ::= A | B | ... | Z

<Description of Computations> ::= BEGIN <List of Assignments> END
<List of Assignments>         ::= <Assignment> | <Assignment> <List of Assignments>
<Assignment>                  ::= <Identifier> = <Expression>;
<Identifier>                  ::= <Letter> <Identifier> | <Letter>
<Expression>                  ::= <Unary Operator> <Subexpression> | <Subexpression>
<Unary Operator>              ::= -
<Subexpression>               ::= (<Expression>) | <Operand> | <Subexpression><Binary Operator><Subexpression>
<Operand>                     ::= <Identifier> | <Constant>
<Binary Operator>             ::= + | - | *


<Constant>                    ::= <Digit> <Constant> | <Digit>
<Digit>                       ::= 0 | 1 | ... | 9

Maximum Identifier Length - 12
